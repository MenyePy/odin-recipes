Bread

Bread image

This bread recipe is the one i know best, obviously i wasnt the one making it but it was my mom, but i kinda get it so please do not try at home unless you are daring because there is still a chance this will work out ;)

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Oil (maybe)
  • Spices (optional)

Steps

  1. Mix 1.5 teaspoon of yeast in water (and set aside)
  2. Melt the butter (and make sure the milk is warm)
  3. Get a bowl and mix all dry ingredients into the bowl.
  4. Dry ingredients: Cinnamon, tumeric, cumin, coriander, baking soda, baking powder, garlic powder, ginger powder, salt, sugar.
  5. Whisk 2 eggs and add into the bowl of dry stuff. (remain a little for later)
  6. Add warm milk into the bowl (also save a little for later)
  7. Add the yeast we made in step one.
  8. Mix Well! and add flour in small batches, mixing every time.
  9. Keep mixing well until you get to the right consistency. (yes, i dont know either, just when it feels right i guess)
  10. When the flour is done roll it into a giant ball and transfer it into another bowl (hopefully a big one) and cover it for rising.
  11. Keep it somewhere for one hour (COVERED PROPERLY!!!!). After an hour, open it.
  12. Knesd the dough and cut it into shapes (i prefer them round, its up to you)
  13. Grease the pan with oil or butter. (Or maybe you have a spray, that's fine too)
  14. Put your bread shapes into the greased pan.
  15. Use a mix of the remaining egg and milk and spread on top of the nread. do not let it touch the pan (if possible)
  16. Set the pan aside for 45 mins to rise again.
  17. After 35 mins, preheat your oven to 375 degrees Farenheit (190C)
  18. Bake for around 45 mins. Usually takes me 1 hour, but just be there until it is done.

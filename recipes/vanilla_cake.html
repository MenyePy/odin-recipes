Vanilla Cake

Vanilla Cake

Ingredients

Source: Youtube (One kind recipe like dat)

  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Oil/Butter
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla essence/extract
  • Flour
  • Baking powder / baking soda
  • Bowl & Blender

Steps

  1. In the blender:
  2. Add milk (1/2 cup)
  3. Add sugar (3/4 cup)
  4. Add 1/2 cup of oil/butter
  5. Add eggs (2)
  6. Add vanilla essence (1 tsp)
  7. Blend (1 minute)
  8. In the bowl:
  9. Sieve in 1 cup of flour and 1/2tsp baking soda + 1/2 tsp baking powder.
  10. You can also use 1tsp baking soda or 1tsp baking powder
  11. Pour the blender mixture on the dry ingredients
  12. Mix thoroughly with a spatula or a whisk.
  13. Grease a pan (with oul, butter, or cooking spray)
  14. Pour the mixture into the greased pan.
  15. Bake dor 30-40 mins (or till golden)

    16. Enjoy!

Pizza

Image of smoothie

This is my pizza recipe, FAR from perfect, but loved by everyone in the fam. It is not so hard to make, and I usually make 4 pizzas at a time. The dough is the same as the bread dough but remove the eggs, so... there's that.

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Yeast
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Oil (for the rising ting, just a lil)
  • Spices (optional)
  • For the toppings:
  • Peppers (Green, red, yellow)
  • Onions
  • Minced meat
  • Tomato stew
  • Peperoni (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the dough. The steps are the same as the steps for bread, except the eggs, but that is for 4 pans. Vary the liquid ingredients to control the number of pans.
  2. Once the dough is in for rising, cut all the ingredients for the toppings. Make some onion rings as well.
  3. MINCED MEAT
  4. Wash the minced meat and leave it to drain in a sieve.
  5. Put a little bit of oil into a pot
  6. Put about 2tbsp of diced onions into the slightly hot oil.
  7. While the onions are burning (lol), get the minced meat ready. It needs as little water in it as possible or else you die.
  8. After about 2-3mins, put the minced meat into the onions. Stir it for about 5 mins.
  9. When you are done stirring it, you need to add spices to it. And salt.
  10. Once you add the spices, let it cook for 2-3mins and go stir it again.
  11. Keep repeating the process of stir and rest until the meat is brown.
  12. Taste the meat and see how bad it is and then cry inside.
  13. Once the meat is done, remove it from the cooker. It will definitely have some water inside, and if possible, you need to sieve it out.
  14. Salad-ish-thing
  15. Put alittlebitta oil into a pan. Any type of pan.
  16. Put in the non-ringy onions first. As they fry, add in all the spices.
  17. Put in the peppers and the onion rings.
  18. Fry them for about 2 mins and then you are done.
  19. If they get soggy, you are a bad chef.
  20. COMBINATION
  21. Now that you have your dough, cut it into 4 equal slices.
  22. Take one of the slices and roll it into the shape of your pan.
  23. Put some cooking spray into the pan and then put the dough inside.
  24. Smear some tomato-stew across the pan.
  25. Put the minced meat into the pan. Then peperoni if you have, then the veggies.
  26. Put the grated cheese on top. If you have sheet cheese, but it before the veggies.
  27. Bake at 375 until done. Takes about 20-40.
  28. Repeat this for all 4 pans.

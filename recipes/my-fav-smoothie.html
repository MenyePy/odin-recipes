My favourite smoothie (Banana/Avocado)

Image of smoothie

This is the smoothie i am used to taking coz i have never taken another one so it is the best automatically and i made it myself totally my idea (dont even sus me) so imma tell y'all how to make it

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Avocado
  • Milk
  • Whatever you want to decorate it with (optional)

Steps

  1. Slice the banana and avocado. (also peel them, important :| )
  2. Put the banana in the blender and grind it because it is easier to blend than avocado.
  3. Put the avocado in and grind everything to a thick paste.
  4. If it is too thick for your liking, add some milk to lighten it.
  5. Serve! (with your decorations [optional])

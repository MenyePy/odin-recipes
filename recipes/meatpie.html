Meat Pie

meat pie image

Ingredients

Source: MAMA

  • About 50g of butter
  • 2 large onions
  • Cooked minced meat
  • 3-5 medium carrots
  • Mint (for flavoring)
  • 5-7 irish potatoes
  • Mushroom (1/4 of a cup) (optional)
  • Flour (5 1/2 cup)
  • 2 eggs
  • Baking powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Spices (optional)

Steps

    Preparations:

  1. Cut the onions, mushrooms, potatoes, and mint.
  2. Low-boil the potatoes until moderately soft.
  3. Cut the carrots (and make sure the minced meat is ready)

    4. Filling:

  4. Put some oil in a pan, and cook the pan on max heat for 1-2 mins (till oil is hot)
  5. Put in the chipped onions.
  6. Put the spices based on your liking.
  7. Put in the mint (optional) and some salt too.
  8. Miz them all in.
  9. Pour in the preboiled potatoes and mix.
  10. Add the minced meat at this point.
  11. If the bottom of the pan is getting sticky, pour in 1cup of warm water, cover it, and let it boil.
  12. As it is boiling, sift 5 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour into a bowl.>
  13. (after sifting) pour the carrots into your filling.
  14. After a few minutes, turn the pan and add a little water.

    15. During that few minutes:

  15. Break 2 eggs into a small bowl.
  16. In another small bowl, mix together some flour and water. Add it to the filling to make it bond together.
  17. After 1-2 mins, turn off the cooker for the filling.
  18. In the flour bowl (it should be big lol), make a hole in the flour and add the eggs.
  19. Cover the eggs in flour (so that it is hidden among the flour)
  20. Sieve in some baking powder (1tbsp), salt (1tsp), and cinnamon (1/2tsp).
  21. Add the butter.
  22. Mix everything with your hands.
  23. If you have any remainder of the flour-water mixture, add it in now. All of it.
  24. Add milk/water till the dough is at a good consistency.

    25. Making a meat pie

  25. Measure out 1/3 cup of dough for each meat pie.
  26. Mold the dough into a ball and roll it into a circle (if possible)
  27. Put it on the meat pie cutter.
  28. Put an adequate amount of filling in it (not too much that it cant close)
  29. Close the meat pie cutter and remove the extra dough.
  30. Repeat this for every single meat pie
  31. Put it in the oven and bake till it's golden brown.
  32. Let it cool down after baking, it is better cold/warm, not hot.

    33. Enjoy!

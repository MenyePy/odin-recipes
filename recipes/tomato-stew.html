Tomato Stew

Tomato stew image (dont worry it wont look like it, it will be better)

Ingredients

Copied straight outta my sisters phone btw :|

  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (Your choice!)
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green pepper
  • Green Peas

Steps

  1. Cut the ginger, garlic, onion, tomato, and green pepper.
  2. Put a pot on fire
  3. Put some oil in and wait for it to heat up
  4. Put in the garlic and ginger and mix
  5. Add in the onion. Mix.
  6. Pit some spices in. We use paprica, tumeric and hot chilli.
  7. Add the tomatoes into the mix and DONT BURN IT!
  8. When it starts boiling pour in the green pepper.
  9. Take it out of the fire. Serve.
  10. Best served on fresh rice
